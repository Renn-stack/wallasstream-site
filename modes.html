Wi-Fi Streaming
Best for: daily use at home / office
Same local Wi-Fi
What you need
  • Your Mac and iPhone on the same local Wi-Fi network
  • 5 GHz recommended
  • Internet access not required (works locally)
How to start
  • Open Wallas’ Stream on Mac
  • Open Wallas’ Stream on iPhone → Wi-Fi Listening
  • Scan the QR code
USB Cable mode
Best for: travel, unstable Wi-Fi
Cable connection
What you need
  • Your iPhone charging cable (USB-C or Lightning)
  • Mac + iPhone connected by cable
  • No Wi-Fi required
How to start
  • Connect your iPhone to your Mac
  • Open Wallas’ Stream on iPhone → USB Cable
  • Open Wallas’ Stream on Mac → USB Cable

Which mode should I use?

If you’re at home, use Wi-Fi. If you want maximum stability or you’re in public networks, use USB Cable mode.

Why 5 GHz matters

5 GHz networks are typically less crowded, which reduces dropouts and makes audio smoother.

Privacy

Wallas’ Stream does not store or upload audio. Streaming happens in real time during active use.
